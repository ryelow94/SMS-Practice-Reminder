Practice Scheduler

This app is intended to help you complete your practice goals for learning an instrument, but can really be used for a multitude of purposes such as learning a language or practicing your coding skills. By entering your phone number, you will receive a text message asking if you have completed your practice goals for the day. You can reply STOP to stop recieving reminders and START to start them again. If you respond 'No' to a practice reminder, the app will remind you 2 minutes from your response. If you respond 'yes' to a reminder, the app will not send you any more notifications until you re-enter your phone number on the webpage.
